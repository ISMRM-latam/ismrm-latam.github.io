The Latin American Chapter of the ISMRM is thrilled to announce our upcoming Business Meeting at the ISMRM & ISMRT Annual Meeting & Exhibition in Honolulu, Hawai!

This meeting is a fantastic opportunity for members of the Latin American MR community to connect, share ideas, and discuss the future of our chapter. We will be discussing upcoming initiatives, elective positions, and ways to get more involved in the chapter’s activities.

Whether you are a long-time member or just curious about what the Latin American Chapter is all about, we invite you to join us for this engaging and informative session. Let's work together to strengthen the MR community in Latin America and beyond!

**Date:** Monday, 06 May 2024
**Time:** 18:15 – 19:15
**Location:** Room 323ABC

We look forward to seeing you there!
