The Latin American Chapter of the ISMRM is pleased to invite you to our upcoming Virtual Meeting. This session will focus on the growth and development of Magnetic Resonance in our region.

We are honored to have two distinguished speakers who have played pivotal roles in the advancement of MR:
* **Pablo Irarrazaval:** Professor at the Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile and Director of the Biomedical Imaging Center.
* **Fernando Calamante:** Professor at the University of Sydney and Director of the National Imaging Facility (NIF) Node at the University of Sydney.

They will share their insights and experiences on the challenges and opportunities for MR research and clinical practice in Latin America. This meeting is an excellent opportunity to learn from experts and engage in discussions about the future of our field.

**Date:** Wednesday, September 4th, 2024
**Time:** 15:00 UTC (12:00 Buenos Aires/Santiago, 10:00 Mexico City/Bogota)

Registration is free for all ISMRM members.
