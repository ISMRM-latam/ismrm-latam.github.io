Join us for a special scientific meeting where we highlight the outstanding work of our chapter members. This event serves as a platform to showcase the diverse and high-impact research being conducted across Latin America.

The meeting will feature a series of short presentations from selected members, covering a wide range of topics in Magnetic Resonance, including:
* Novel sequence development
* Clinical applications in neurology and cardiology
* Advances in MR hardware and low-field imaging
* AI and machine learning in image reconstruction

This is a unique opportunity to catch up on the latest scientific trends in the region and to foster collaborations between different research groups. We encourage all students and trainees to attend and engage with the presenters.
