The Latin American Chapter is dedicated to supporting the next generation of MR scientists. This session is specifically designed for trainees, PhD students, and post-docs.

The "ISMRM Trainee" meeting will focus on professional development and networking. Agenda items include:
* **Meet the Mentors:** A breakout session where students can talk to established PIs about career paths in academia and industry.
* **Grant Writing Tips:** Advice on applying for ISMRM travel awards and regional research funding.
* **Trainee Representative Update:** A report from our Trainee Representative on current and future initiatives for students within the chapter.

We want to hear from you! This meeting will include an open forum for students to suggest activities and resources that the chapter can provide to support their growth.
