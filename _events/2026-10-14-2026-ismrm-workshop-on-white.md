This interdisciplinary workshop explores advanced MRI methods for studying white matter, covering validation, microstructural modeling, quantitative imaging, AI-based analysis, and clinical applications. It brings together brain and spinal cord researchers and clinicians to discuss emerging techniques, translational research, and the clinical value of quantitative MRI, while fostering collaboration and networking.
