The Latin American Chapter of the International Society for Magnetic Resonance in Medicine (ISMRM) operates under the following bylaws to ensure the promotion of scientific collaboration and education in the region.

### Article I: Name and Purpose
The name of this organization shall be the Latin American Chapter of the International Society for Magnetic Resonance in Medicine. Its primary purpose is to promote communication and collaboration among professionals in MR within Latin America and with the global community.

### Article II: Membership
Membership is open to any member of the ISMRM in good standing who resides or works in Latin America, or who has a professional interest in the development of MR in the region.

### Article III: Governing Committee
The Chapter shall be governed by a committee consisting of:
* Chair
* Vice-Chair
* Secretary
* Treasurer
* Communications Officer
* Trainee Representative

### Article IV: Elections and Terms
Officers are elected by the membership. Terms generally last for one to two years, ensuring a rotation of leadership that represents the geographic diversity of the region.

### Article V: Meetings
An annual business meeting will be held, typically in conjunction with the ISMRM Annual Meeting, alongside virtual meetings throughout the year to manage chapter initiatives.
