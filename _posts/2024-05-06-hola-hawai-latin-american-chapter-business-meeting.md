During the **ISMRM 2024 Annual Meeting & Exhibition** in Honolulu, Hawaii, the Latin American Chapter held its inaugural in-person business meeting. The room was filled with energy as members from across the continent and the diaspora gathered to set the roadmap for our first year.

### Key Highlights:
* **Regional Workshops:** Plans were discussed for upcoming "Best of ISMRM-LATAM" workshops to be held in various South American cities to share knowledge with those unable to travel to Hawaii.
* **Travel Grants:** The committee introduced a plan to lobby for increased travel support specifically for researchers coming from Latin American institutions.
* **Collaboration Network:** A new initiative was proposed to map MR hardware and expertise across the region (e.g., specific coils, low-field MRI, and Halbach array research) to facilitate multi-center studies.

### Committee Roadmap:
The Governing Committee presented their goals for 2024-2025, focusing on:
1. Increasing the visibility of LATAM research in major journals.
2. Building a directory of educational resources in Spanish and Portuguese.
3. Strengthening ties with other regional chapters.

Mahalo to everyone who joined us in Honolulu. We look forward to seeing the growth of this community in the coming months!
