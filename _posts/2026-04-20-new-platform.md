We are thrilled to announce the official launch of the new web platform for the ISMRM Latin American Chapter! 

As our community continues to grow, we recognized the need for a digital space that is as dynamic, collaborative, and open as the researchers who make up our chapter. Built on GitHub Pages, this new site is designed with a core philosophy in mind: **transparency and community-driven growth.**

Here is a look at the major new features we have rolled out:

### 🔄 Distributed, Community-Driven Updates
Gone are the days of sending emails to a webmaster and waiting weeks for an update. We have transitioned to a fully distributed update system. 

Anyone in the community can now ask to include an event, share news, post a job opportunity, or add themselves to the member directory simply by submitting an **Issue** directly on our GitHub repository. Once submitted, the ISMRM Latam Board will quickly review and approve the request. This workflow ensures that our platform remains up-to-date, transparent, and built by the community, for the community.

### 📍 Interactive Member Map
Latin America is vast, and our researchers are spread across the globe. To help us stay connected, we have launched a brand-new **Member Community Map**. Every registered member is represented on this interactive map, allowing you to easily find colleagues by country, explore their specific research interests, and read a brief bio. It has never been easier to discover who is working on what in our region.

### 🔬 Labs & Institutions Directory
Fostering multi-center collaboration is a primary goal of our chapter. To support this, we have introduced a dedicated map and directory for MR labs and institutions. If your lab wants to increase its visibility and connect with potential collaborators or students, simply submit an update issue to get your institution pinned on the map. 

### 💼 Real-Time Job Opportunities
Looking for your next PhD candidate, postdoc, or a new career opportunity? Our new Job Opportunities board prioritizes speed and transparency. You no longer need to wait for a newsletter to go out; just submit an issue with the job details, and upon review, it will be immediately available to the entire network.

***

**Ready to get involved?**
The platform is live and waiting for your input. We encourage you to head over to our GitHub repository, open an issue, and add yourself to the new member map today! 

Let's continue building a stronger, more connected MR community in Latin America.
